## About Me

I am a researcher working on neural rendering, 3D reconstruction, and computational photography. I received my Ph.D. from the Max Planck Institute for Informatics, where I was advised by [Prof. Karol Myszkowski](https://people.mpi-inf.mpg.de/~karol/) and [Prof. Hans-Peter Seidel](https://people.mpi-inf.mpg.de/~hpseidel/). I also worked closely with [Prof. Ana Serrano](https://ana-serrano.github.io/) and [Dr. Thomas Leimkühler](https://people.mpi-inf.mpg.de/~tleimkue/). My Ph.D. thesis received an Honorable Mention for the [Eurographics PhD Award](https://www.eg.org/wp/eurographics-awards-programme/phd-award/).
I received the M.S. degree in Computer Engineering from Peking University in 2020, advised by [Prof. Ronggang Wang](https://www.ece.pku.edu.cn/info/1046/2147.htm), and the B.S. degree in Information Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2017. I was a Research Scientist Intern at Adobe's Next Cam Lab from July 2024 to September 2024, working with [Marc Levoy](https://graphics.stanford.edu/~levoy/) and [Cecilia Zhang](https://ceciliavision.github.io/). Prior to that, I interned as an Algorithm Research Scientist at Tencent Robotics X Lab from December 2019 to June 2020, under the mentorship of [Yonggen Ling](https://ygling2008.github.io/). I was also an research assitant at the [Spatial Intelligence Lab, University Of Calgary](https://ucalgary.ca/labs/spatial-intelligence/home) from May 2016 to September 2016, advised by [Prof. Ruisheng Wang](https://profiles.ucalgary.ca/ruisheng-wang).

## Research Interests

- **Computer Vision:** image generation, computational photography
- **Computer Graphics:** nerual rendering, material appearance perception.
